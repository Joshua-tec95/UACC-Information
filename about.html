Our Mission

The Universal Associated Christian College(UACC) is a mission focused partnership program of the Associated brotherhood of Christian and the Universal Church of Christ Inc. We aim to build and foster academic execellence providing a four years school of Technology, Christian Education and Nursing. Through an exemplary nurturing of the spiritual life of the student, will result in being Christ servant who is thoroughly furnished in the word of God, is focused on living and correctly teaching it; ministering to the total man. The program of study at the College will culminate in acquiring Bachelor of Theology Degree (B.Th.), Bachelor of Christian Education (B.Eds.) and Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSc).

Brief History

To be a leading institution of higher learning that cultivates intellectual excellence, moral integrity, and spiritual growth. We envision a community where students flourish as scholars, leaders, and global citizens committed to service and social responsibility.
