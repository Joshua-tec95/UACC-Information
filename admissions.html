Admissions

Start Your Journey at UACC

Application Process

1

Submit Application

Complete our online application form with personal and educational information.

2

Provide Documentation

Submit official transcripts, test scores, and letters of recommendation.

3

Interview

Participate in an interview with an admissions counselor (optional but recommended).

4

Receive Decision

Get your admission decision within 1-2 weeks of completing your application.

5

Enroll & Register

Confirm enrollment and register for classes in your chosen program.

Application Requirements

Undergraduate Requirements

  • ✓ High school diploma or GED
  • ✓ Official high school transcript
  • ✓ Standardized test scores (SAT/ACT)
  • ✓ Letter of recommendation (1-2)
  • ✓ Personal essay or statement
  • ✓ Application fee ($50)
